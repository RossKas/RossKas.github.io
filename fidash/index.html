WHO WE ARE

We help women look great everyday. Fidah is a personal styling product. We use sofisticated technology to learn about your style choices and fashion habits. Our stylists work closely with you to create outfits and make wardrobe recommendations that suit your preference and lifestyle.

HOW IT WORKS

STEP 1
Take a break!
Take the Style Quiz.

Register with us. We send you a style quiz. This is our very step of understanding your style tastes and basic preferences.

STEP 2
We do the work.
We create outfits for you.

Our stylists start put together personalized outfit plans per your style, weather conditions and budget. We send them on your phone. You can choose to buy from our recommendations.

STEP 3
Earn wardrobe credits.
Upload wardrobe photos.

Get more outfit recommendations by uploading photos of clothes you already own. Collect wardrobe credits for uploads. Receive benefits for shopping from sustainable brands.
