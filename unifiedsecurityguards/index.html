TOP 3 REASONS TO HIRE SECURITY GUARDS

VISUAL THEFT DETERRANT

While digital surveillance and staff vigilance play important roles in theft deterrance, nothing matches the impact that an on-site security officer can have have on reducing shrink.

The presence of an alert and attentive security guard gives would-be criminals significant pause, because they can visualize the direct repurcussions of their planned actions. Simply put, thieves would much rather operate in an environment free of security guards.

INSTANT RESPONSE

Video cameras and other tools used in theft prevention are lacking one thing that makes them truly useful - direct action and direct response.

Without action, without response, surveillance is much more limited in its ability to create positive outcomes. When incidents are unfolding on-site, the best way to bring order to chaos is action in the moment. Our security officers are professionally trained to keep your place of business secure.

ON-SITE RESOLUTIONS

The best resolutions are resolutions that occur on-site. It is far better to correct a situation as it unfolds than to deal with witness accounts after the fact. ‘He said, she said’ is not even a factor when the event is being handled instantly.

In addition to the swift responses and professional resolutions that our security guards can provide, they possess two things missing in solitary digital solutions: intuition and wisdom.
