WHO WE ARE

Fidah is an on-demand pocket stylist that creates everyday looks using your existing wardrobe. It enables mindful purchases by finding new items match your style and work with your existing wardrobe - saving you time, money and effort while reducing waste. With Fidah you not only look amazing but also champion sustainable fashion in style!

HOW IT WORKS

STEP 1
Take a break!
Take the Style Quiz.

In order for us to understand your needs and aspirations better, we require you to complete a 5 minute style quiz. It’s short and fun

STEP 2
We do the work.
We create outfits for you.

Once we understand your lifestyle, we create outfits for you keeping in mind your schedule and weather.

STEP 3
Earn wardrobe credits.
Upload wardrobe photos.

You get additional outfit ideas by uploading photos of your wardrobe items. We’ll use them in your looks and you will earn points to spend on Fidah add-ons
